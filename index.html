AHD CAMERAS

AHD stands for Analog High Definition.AHD is an analog closed-circuit television video surveillance standard of high definition 720P video which allows the camera to send an HD signal over a standard coax or RCA cable.

DIGITAL VIDEO RECORDER(DVR)

A Digital Video Recorder (DVR) records video to local storage devices, most commonly a hard drive. DVR can record analog video sources on-site or capture video from a digital source. DVRs can be connected to analog cameras via coaxial cables, enabling them to be accessed remotely.

IP CAMERAS

An IP camera, or Internet protocol camera, is a type of digital security camera that receives and sends video footage via an IP network.
